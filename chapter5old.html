About This Project

This website was created as a project for a computer science class.

The topic chosen for the project is a quick overview of the most basic steps of adult CPR.

The goal of the site is to present simple CPR concepts in a clear and easy-to-read format.

This project was created quickly and somewhat on the fly as part of the learning process while building the website.

The information included covers only the very basic aspects of CPR and should not be considered complete medical training.

If you are interested in learning CPR properly, consider taking a certified CPR course from a qualified training organization.

Adult CPR Quick Guide

Steps for Performing Adult CPR
Step Procedure Purpose Important Notes
Action Details
1 Check Responsiveness Tap shoulders and shout Determine if person is conscious Look for movement or response
2 Call Emergency Services Call 911 or ask someone nearby Get professional help quickly Do not delay CPR
3 Check Breathing Look for chest movement Determine if CPR is needed No breathing = start CPR
4 Begin Compressions Push hard and fast in center of chest Circulate blood Depth at least 2 inches
5 Compression Rate 100–120 compressions per minute Maintain blood flow Keep a steady rhythm
6 Use AED Apply as soon as available Restore heart rhythm Follow device instructions
