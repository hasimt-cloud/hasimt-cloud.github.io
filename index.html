Welcome

This website introduces the basic steps of cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR). CPR is a lifesaving technique used when a person’s heart stops beating or they stop breathing normally.

Acting quickly can help maintain blood flow and oxygen to the brain until emergency medical professionals arrive.
