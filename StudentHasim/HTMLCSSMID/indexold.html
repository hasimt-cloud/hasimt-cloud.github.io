Welcome

This website introduces the basic steps of cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR). CPR is a lifesaving technique used when a person’s heart stops beating or they stop breathing normally. Acting quickly can help maintain blood flow and oxygen to the brain until emergency medical professionals arrive. This guide explains the key steps of CPR, including checking responsiveness, calling emergency services, beginning chest compressions, and continuing care until help arrives.

What You Will Learn

  • How to check if a person is responsive
  • When and how to call emergency services
  • How to perform chest compressions
  • The importance of using an AED when available
  • How to continue CPR until help arrives
