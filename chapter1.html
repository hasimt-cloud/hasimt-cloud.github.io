Checking Responsiveness

Learn how to determine if someone needs CPR.

Why This Step Matters

Before starting CPR, you must determine if the person is conscious and breathing normally.

Steps

Tap Shoulders

Tap the person's shoulders firmly to check for responsiveness.

Shout

Shout loudly: "Are you okay?" and look for any response.

Check Breathing

Look for breathing or movement in the chest.

Call for Help

If there is no response, call emergency services immediately.
